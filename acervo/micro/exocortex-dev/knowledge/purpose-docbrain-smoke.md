### DocBrain — Purpose

> This file defines WHY this knowledge base exists.

### Mission

Build and maintain a **persistent, LLM-maintained personal knowledge base** that:

1. Ingests documents of unknown formats and conditions
2. Extracts structured knowledge with full source traceability
3. Organizes content for efficient LLM consumption
4. Maintains freshness and accuracy through automated linting
5. Provides a web-based scraping engine for real-time information

### Principles

1. **Provenance First** — Every piece of knowledge traces back to its source (citation, date, expiry)
2. **LLM-Friendly** — Content optimized for machine reading (dense paragraphs, structured frontmatter)
3. **Human-Navigable** — Obsidian-compatible wiki with [[wikilinks]] and graph view
4. **Fallback-Safe** — Core functions work without LLM; LLM enhances structure quality
5. **Compounding** — Knowledge builds on itself; cross-references grow over time

### Non-Goals

- Not a search engine (we curate, not index)
- Not a chatbot (the wiki IS the output, not conversations)
- Not a database (markdown files are the source of truth)
